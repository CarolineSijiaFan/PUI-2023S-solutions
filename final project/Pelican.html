Pelican App

Mobile service application designed for Pelican watersports aiming to solve the first penguin problem.

Time: Feb 2023 - Mar 2023

Teammates: Gabriella Howse, Tasha Russman

Current Problem

Kayak beginners are afraid of start kayaking as they are the first penguins among their friends. Hence, Pelican Watersports couldn't retain the kayak beginners.

Goal

Create an application that give various kayaking tips and encourage them keep kayaking. Hence, we would be able to retain the Pelican users.
