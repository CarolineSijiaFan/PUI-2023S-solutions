BetterNotes

All-in-one note-taking application. Improve the note-taking experience while also teach students to take better notes.

Time: Feb 2023 - May 2023

Teammates: Yiming Zhao, Mahirah Alam

Current Problem

1.

Studnets don't have a real "all-in-one" digital note-taking application.

2.

Students don't know how to take digital notes properly.

3.

Students can get distracted while taking digital notes.

However, note-taking is an important part of every students' learning.

Goal

Create a note-taking application that is suitable for all subjects.

The application should also be able to teach students to take better notes.

Highlight #1
Focus Mode
In-app block notifications function that help students keep focused while taking notes.
 
Highlight #2
Note Academy
Online community where the students could learn more about note-taking techniques. Students can also share their notes with others.
 
Highlight #3
Organize Notes
Teach students to have better note taking habites. For example, turn longer notes into bullet points.
 
Highlight #4
Note Templates
Better note functions for different courses. For example, record your important code tracing problems for your CS courses!
 
Highlight #5
Module Outline
Guide the students to review their notes in forms of mindmap, timelines, etc. Remind the students of the concepts they forget about.
